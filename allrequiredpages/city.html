Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to commercial repair requests?

We aim to provide fast service, with many customers receiving a response within hours. For urgent situations, same-day repairs are often available depending on technician availability.

Do you repair both gas and electric cooking appliances?

Yes. Our technicians are trained to handle gas and electric ovens, stoves, and ranges safely and efficiently.

Is maintenance really necessary for kitchen appliances?

Regular maintenance helps prevent unexpected breakdowns, improves performance, and extends appliance lifespan. It’s especially important for commercial kitchens.

Do you provide repair services for large commercial kitchens?

Absolutely. We specialize in commercial-grade appliance repairs for restaurants, hotels, bakeries, and catering businesses.

Are your repairs covered by any service guarantee?

Yes. We stand behind our workmanship and use quality parts to ensure long-lasting results.
